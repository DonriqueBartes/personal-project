Sculptured Acrylic

Sculptured Acrylic sets are strong and durable nail extensions built on the natural nail using nail forms as a base for the extension. Sculptures always feel light on your nail as the application of the acrylic will follow your nail’s natural growth pattern while creating a desired length and shape of your choice. Not recommended for clients who work with water/chemicals/detergents. *Please note: Acrylic treatments include base and top coat but EXCLUDE gel colour and/or any nail art.

Acrylic Overlay

Acrylic is applied on top of the length of the natural nail, without forms, to reinforce brittle/weak nails and act as a protective layer while still keeping a natural look to the nail. Overlays are recommended to clients who’s nails tend to chip/break easily. Not recommended for clients who work with water/chemicals/detergents. *Please note: Acrylic treatments include base and top coat but EXCLUDE gel colour and/or any nail art.

Ombre Sculpted Acrylic

Also known as ‘baby boomer’ or ‘french fade’ nails, ombre sculptures are durable nail extensions built on the natural nail using forms as a base for the extension with a pink-to-white fade created in the acrylic. Sculptures always feel light on your nail as the application of the acrylic will follow your nail’s natural growth pattern while creating a desired length and shape of your choice. Not recommended for clients who work with water/chemicals/detergents. *Please note: Acrylic treatments include base and top coat but EXCLUDE gel colour and/or any nail art.

Mini Manicure/Pedicure

Soak, filing, cuticle work, buff and shine and/or paint. *Please note: the paint offered in this treatment is the Orly Breathable Nail Polish and does not include gel and nail art
